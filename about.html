About Eve

Eve is a bog witch who splits her time between her cozy cottage on the web and various cranberry bogs in the Pacific Northwest. She enjoys pictures, colors, and words in various forms.

Eve's speciality is web witchery: removing hexes and curses from websites and replacing them with charms and protective enchantments.
